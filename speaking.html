Keynotes for Leaders
Under Pressure.

Not motivational. Not theatrical. Structured thinking — delivered from lived experience at the highest level of performance.

What to Expect

Every keynote is built around a single, testable idea.

I don't perform insight. I share what I have lived — from Olympic campaigns to executive boardrooms — and translate it into frameworks leaders can use the following day.


My delivery is measured. Deliberate. The room doesn't get energised.


It gets clarified.

Core Keynotes

Six keynotes. One consistent thread.

Flagship

Protecting Decision Quality Under Pressure

A direct exploration of the Pressure Clarity Framework — what happens when perspective narrows, why it is predictable, and how leaders can intervene before performance collapses.

Best for: Boards navigating governance under pressure.

Preventing Perspective Collapse

A focused keynote on the internal fracture pattern that precedes most leadership failures. Practical, immediate, and grounded in elite-level experience.

Best for: Leadership conferences at strategy inflection points.

Alignment Prevents Collapse

How misalignment compounds quietly under pressure — and what structurally aligned teams do differently when stakes are high.

Best for: Executive teams leading through volatility.

Cold Clarity vs Constructive Clarity

The distinction between clarity that protects trust and clarity that destroys it. A keynote for leaders navigating hard conversations at pace.

Best for: High-growth organizations scaling culture.

The Cost of Control Under Load

Why tightening control under pressure is the most common and most expensive leadership error — and what widening perspective looks like in practice.

Best for: Senior partners in professional services firm.

Designing Cultures That Hold Under Pressure

How environment shapes decision quality — and how to build the kind of culture that doesn't fracture when volatility increases.

Best for: Performance directors in elite sport or enterprise.
